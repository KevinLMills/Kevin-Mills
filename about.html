About Me

My background, hobbies, and future plans.

Background

I grew up in the Boise metro area, spending much of my time as a kid playing soccer. In middle school, I started in band playing the saxophone. When in high school, I had to balance my time between band and soccer at all times. Eventually, I swapped to Cross-country and had a much better time balancing those.

After graduating from Kuna High School in the spring of 2023, I moved up to Moscow to attend the University of Idaho. Here, I began studying computer science with hopes of pursuing a career in software engineering. I've been participating in various ensembles here, mostly the Jazz bands and Marching band.

-Hobbies Quick List-

  • Saxophone & Jazz
  • Mellophone and DCI
  • Soccer & Cross-country
  • Video Games, primarily Pokemon

Future Plans

After I graduate, I hope to find a job in software engineering. I have always had a passion for building tools and working in teams. Web development is a newly found passion of mine that I have had lots of fun with so far. I plan to move with my girlfriend to a city in the Northwest US wherever a job opens up. The Portland area is at the top of our list, though I do really love the Boise area.
